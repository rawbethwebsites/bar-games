## Variant: Neon Arcade

### Design stance
Arcade duel — neon glow, split red/blue energy, futuristic. Makes it feel like a competitive video game.

### Key choices
- **Layout:** Dark panel with ambient red/blue glow.
- **Typography:** Orbitron for headings (arcade/sci-fi), Inter for body.
- **Color:** Deep space black, neon red vs neon blue, gold as accent.
- **Interaction:** Glow intensifies on hover.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: instant "this is a game" energy, buzzer duels feel electric.
- Weak at: less connected to the courtroom/law theme; may feel cheap if overdone.

### Best for
A younger crowd or after-dinner party energy where laughs and competition matter more than ceremony.
