About

The Bar Games is a two-player courtroom party game built for bars, parties, and head-to-head duels.

The big screen (PC or laptop) is the courtroom — it shows the cases, scores, and verdicts. Your phone is your buzzer — tap to answer, buzz in, or lock your choice.

Works from anywhere — your phone just needs internet, no same Wi-Fi required.
